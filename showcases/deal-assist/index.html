The Deal Assist Operator

The CEO Deal Assist operator reads the Pipeline Reality output each week and identifies the 2–3 deals where executive involvement can change an outcome. It generates paste-ready intervention materials — executive messages in multiple tonal variants, internal AE tee-ups, and CRM update suggestions — so the CEO can act in minutes, not hours.
